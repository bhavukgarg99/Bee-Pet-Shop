Our Specialists

James – Daycare Supervisor

James ensures a safe and fun environment for all pets. He is dedicated to creating meaningful play sessions and often worries about each pet’s well-being when they're not in his sight. His goal is to make sure no pet feels left out or lonely.

Sophia – Lead Dog Walker

Sophia is an experienced dog walker who tailors each walk to your dog’s individual needs. She often feels guilty about pets like Max who stay alone during work hours and strives to provide nurturing extra care and attention to ease their loneliness.

Olivia – Grooming Expert

Olivia is skilled in all aspects of pet grooming, from baths to haircuts. She understands owners' worries about finding trustworthy grooming services and is committed to providing a stress-free and enjoyable experience for both pets and their owners.
